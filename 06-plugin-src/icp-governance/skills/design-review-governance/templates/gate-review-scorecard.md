### Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 5 | Fully met — no issues |
| 4 | Substantially met — minor gaps, manageable |
| 3 | Partially met — gaps exist, remediation plan needed |
| 2 | Significant gaps — major remediation required |
| 1 | Not met — fundamental issues |

### Thresholds

| Weighted Score | Decision |
|---|---|
| >= 4.0 | Go — proceed to next phase |
| 3.0 - 3.9 | Conditional Go — proceed with action items, re-review in {n} weeks |
| < 3.0 | No-Go — resolve issues and re-submit for gate review |
