# ADR-003: Engineering Automation Domain

**Status:** Accepted
**Date:** 2026-03-13
**Context:** Phase 1 Domain Map Design

## Decision

Include Engineering Automation (D13) as a Level 1 domain covering automated calculation tools, AI-assisted engineering, CI/CD for engineering deliverables, and workflow automation.

## Rationale

ICP is actively developing automation capabilities for engineering processes. These skills are cross-cutting (they serve all other domains) but require distinct competencies (software development, AI/ML, DevOps). Grouping them as a standalone domain enables clear ownership and investment tracking for the automation initiative.

## Consequences

- D13 has 4 subdomains covering tools, AI, CI/CD, and workflow automation
- Skills in D13 are primarily "meta-skills" that enhance other domains' efficiency
- Lower initial candidate count (6) reflects emerging maturity; expected to grow significantly
